Cuisine

Cuisine is a lively and vibrant template for Freeway Pro. Images are given pride of place, with a space on each page for a beautifully tempting full width shot. It’s flexible, fully editable, and would work well as a website for a café, restaurant, or other similar establishments.

Preview

Responsive Layout

The layout automatically adjusts to fit any device, making it look great on mobiles, tablets, and desktops alike.

Google Fonts

The fonts in this template have been specially selected. Nothing is required to use them, just publish your site, sit back, and enjoy.

Full Width Images

Give your images the star treatment. They'll look fantastic in this template stretching the full width of the browser window.

Responsive Contact Form

A responsive contact form has been included. All you need to do is add the email address you want it to be sent to.

Customized Google Maps

This template features a Google Map that has been customized to fit the look and feel of the rest of the site.
