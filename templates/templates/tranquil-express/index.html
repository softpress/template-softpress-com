Tranquil

Tranquil is a dark and stylish template for Freeway Express. The images provided make it perfect for a garden landscaping company site, but with new images and colors it would also work very well as a site for other outdoor service based sites.

Preview

Beautiful Layout

This template makes use of multiple column layouts along with a perfect balance of typography and images.

Gorgeous Typography

The fonts in this template have been specially selected. Nothing is required to use them, just publish your site, sit back, and enjoy.

Full Screen Background Image

A striking image has been fixed in position in the background of the page, creating an impactful but not overly imposing effect.

Social Media Buttons

This template comes with a complete set of social media rollover buttons ready to hook-up to your networks.
